### Protecting Your Digital Footprint

Smartphones are vital tools for safety, but they can also leak location and personal data if not configured securely. Here are key settings every user should check:

1. **Location Permissions**: Only allow apps to access your location "While Using the App." Avoid giving "Always Allow" permissions unless it is a trusted safety tracking app.
2. **Wi-Fi and Bluetooth**: Turn off automatic connection to public Wi-Fi networks, which can be intercepted by malicious third parties.
3. **Emergency SOS**: Configure your device's built-in SOS shortcut (e.g., clicking the power button 5 times on iOS or Android) to instantly alert contacts and share location.

### App Audit Checklist

Perform a monthly check on your device:
- Remove unused apps that might run in the background.
- Check which apps have access to your contacts, camera, and microphone.
- Use secure messaging services like Signal or WhatsApp with end-to-end encryption.
